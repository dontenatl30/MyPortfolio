PuzzleCrafts

PuzzleCrafts is an engaging web application meticulously crafted with a tech stack featuring Node.js, Express, Sequelize, ElephantSQL, JavaScript, JWT, React, and Bootstrap. Users can register, log in, and explore a variety of themed puzzles with adjustable difficulty levels, ensuring an exciting and personalized experience. Upon successfully completing a puzzle, users unlock intriguing riddles, providing an extra layer of entertainment. The application also empowers users to manage their profiles, enhancing the overall interactive journey. PuzzleCrafts showcases my proficiency in full-stack development and my dedication to creating captivating and user-centric web applications.
